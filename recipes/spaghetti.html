Spaghetti and meatsauce

Gather 'round for this classic dish!

You can't go wrong with this traditional favorite! This is a quick, easy, and hearty meal that the whole family will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • One package spaghetti noodles
  • One pound 80% Lean Ground Beef
  • One jar of Ragu Traditional pasta sauce
  • Freshly grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a greased frying pan, heat ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Drain grease and set aside.
  2. Heat water until boiling in a six quart pot.
  3. When water is boiling, break spaghetti noodles in half and insert into pot, cooking for eight to ten minutes, or until pasta is tender.
  4. While noodles are boiling, return frying pan to medium heat and add pasta sauce to meat. Heat until sauce begins to bubble, stirring occasional.
  5. Combine noodles and sauce mixture. Serve with grated parmesan cheese to taste.
