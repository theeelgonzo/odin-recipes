Cauliflower Casserole

Loaded With Love

Here's the best thing about this recipe...it's got the best of both worlds! Nice healthy cauliflower, elbow to elbow with gooey cheese and super crispy bacon! This is a savory dish for any occasion, and works perfectly paired with any kind of meat for an entree! Check it out!

Ingredients

  • Frozen Cauliflower
  • Bacon
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Green Onions
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cook the bacon and set aside
  3. Boil the frozen cauliflower until tender
  4. Grease a 2 quart oven safe baking dish
  5. Spread a layer of cheese on the bottom of the dish
  6. On top of the cheese, build a layer of cauliflower, bacon, and green onions.
  7. Top the casserole with more the rest of the cheese
  8. Insert in oven and bake for 25 minutes, or until cheese is golden brown
  9. Let sit for two minutes, and serve hot!
