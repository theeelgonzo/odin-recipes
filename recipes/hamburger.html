Stovetop Hamburgers

It's Burger Time

Here's a quick and easy way to enjoy this traditional American favorite meal. With a little prep time this is an easy meal to make on a busy weekday evening, and can easily be transferred to the grill instead of stovetop for a weekend barbecue!

Ingredients

  • 5 Pounds Raw Ground Beef
  • One medium onion, red or white according to your taste
  • Table Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Sliced Mushrooms
  • One Clove of Garlic, minced
  • One Tomato, sliced
  • Iceberg Lettuce

Steps

  1. Place the ground beef in a large mixing bowl
  2. Season the beef with salt and pepper
  3. Dice the onion, then fold the onion into the beef
  4. Grease a skillet lightly, then heat it on the stove top to medium-high heat
  5. Form patties by hand from the beef, approximately three and a half to four inches in diameter, and an inch or less thick
  6. Cook on the skillet to preference. For medium done, approximately three minutes on either side
  7. When patties are cooked as desired, serve on brioche buns and garnish with a slice of tomato, some lettuce, and mushrooms as needed
